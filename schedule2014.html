                • Interested in joining?

                  Cal Women's Water Polo Club welcomes all UC Berkeley female students - undergrads or grads! All abilities are welcome! Our team has a wide variety of skill, talent, and experience, ranging from former NCAA D1 athletes to individuals who are brand new to the sport.
